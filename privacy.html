Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

Information We Collect

When you use the Outhouse Races Bracket Challenge app, we collect:

  • Email address (required for account creation and prize notifications)
  • Bracket predictions and picks
  • Power-up purchases through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Tournament statistics (points, standings, raffle tickets)
  • Tiebreaker time prediction

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your bracket and tournament participation
  • Calculate standings and award prizes
  • Conduct the raffle drawing
  • Process power-up purchases
  • Contact winners via email
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using Firebase/Firestore
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Payment processing is handled securely by Apple
  • Your data is retained for the duration of the tournament and may be used for future events

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Update your email address
  • Opt out of future communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@liquidblueevents.com

Third-Party Services

This app uses:

  • Firebase/Firestore for data storage
  • Apple In-App Purchases for power-up transactions

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
